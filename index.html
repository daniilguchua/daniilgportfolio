Proficient in Python, JavaScript (JS), and React, I develop software solutions and contribute meaningfully to engineering teams.

My Story

Minecraft was more than just a game; it was a lifeline during my challenging childhood in Saint Petersburg, Russia. At the age of 10, my friend Deema introduced me to this immersive world, igniting my passion for engineering and design. Through this, I discovered a sense of belonging and stability, which helped me navigate personal struggles and embrace my true self. This experience underscored the transformative power of technology and the importance of diverse perspectives in driving innovation.

My Passions

In the future, my passion is to be able to collaborate with a team of like-minded individuals to engineer solutions into the world that might have a similar impact on another person's life as Minecraft had on mine. It would be an honor and a dream to have the poetically full-circle opportunity to contribute to something bigger than myself and contribute to innovative solutions/designs that can change the world.
